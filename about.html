About Hotel Royal Stay


Royal Stay Guesthouse is a budget-friendly accommodation located just a short walk from the famous Charminar. Its prime location offers easy access to popular attractions like the INS Kurusura Submarine Museum and the TU 142 Aircraft Museum, making it an ideal choice for tourists.


Established as a cozy guesthouse, Royal Stay Guesthouse has been serving travelers seeking comfortable and affordable accommodation near the scenic Ramakrishna Beach. Over the years, it has become a preferred choice for both leisure and business travelers due to its prime location and hospitable services. We are providing Air-conditioned rooms with geysers , Free Wi-Fi , 24-hour front desk , Room service ,Parking facilities.
